Mobile care path

Mobile care path for patients undergoing surgical operation

KYS mobile care path offers information and support for patient waiting for operaton and afterwords. It provides a secure communication system between patient and healthcare. The interactive timeline in the app provides patients with instructions precisely when they need them. The chronological order of events makes it easy for patients to follow everything that needs to be completed thourough the care path. The app was developed together with Buddy HealthCare.

Some of the key ideas, lessons and working methods of this project: modeling and digitizing the process of surgical patients, expertise work, collaboration with various stakeholders, workshop-based work, collecting feedback from clients, continuous development. Additionally: privacy practices, accessibility, testing, validation, documentation, publishing the mobile application in app store.

IMS Process

Process modeling enables better analysis and development of healthcare operations

At the Kuopio University Hospital, a surgical handbook project was carried out during the years 2022-2023, during which we documented and developed the pre-, peri-, and post-operative care of surgical patients. Throughout the project, in multidisciplinary development workshops, we increased understanding, standardized procedures, and devised improvement measures to enhance surgical operations. For process documentation, we utilized the Arter IMS process modeling software available in our region, along with a guidelines management system.

Some of the key ideas, lessons and working methods of this project: facilitation of workgroups and virtual facilitation using the Howspace platform, working with Miro boards, establishment and collaboration with the Surgery Customer Advisory Board.

Later, in relation to this, I have learned process mining and automation using Uipath and Celonis environments.

Digital carepath

Health Village My Path is a digital service channel for healthcare and social welfare

At Kuopio University Hospital and later on at Wellbeing Services County of North Savo, we have been building digital care pathways for several specialties. The platform utilized has been Health Village My Path. I have been involved in developing a digital care pathway for patients with severe heart conditions, for patients with coronary artery disease, and most recently for residents in our well-being area with vascular diseases. The development has always involved a multidisciplinary working group, as well as actively engaging patients.

Some of the key ideas, lessons and working methods of this project: he digitalization of care pathways, development and elaboration of digital care pathways on the SharePoint platform, considering accessibility, customer participation, establishment and collaboration with Customer Advisory Board regarding digitalization, customer feedback, digital skills training, facilitation of workshops, writing blogs, data protection, process approval, testing, and monitoring related to medical devices.

Heart Hub in Health Village

Health Village offers information and support for everyone, care for patients and tools for professionals

Health Village is a public online service developed by Finnish university hospitals. Its content is produced by social and healthcare professionals. HealthVillage.fi contains 33 virtual hubs built around different themes. During my project, content related to heart failure was produced for the Heart Disease Hub. Additionally, within the framework of Health Village development and the Heart Disease Hub, national collaboration continues.
